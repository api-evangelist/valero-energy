# Valero Energy

Valero Energy Corporation is the world's largest independent petroleum refiner and a leading producer of low-carbon transportation fuels. Headquartered in San Antonio, Texas, Valero operates 14 petroleum refineries with 3 million barrels per day capacity, 12 ethanol plants, and renewable diesel production facilities across the US, Canada, and UK.

**Human URL:** [https://www.valero.com/](https://www.valero.com/)

**Stock Symbol:** NYSE: VLO | Fortune 100

## Description

Valero is a Fortune 500 company that transforms crude oil and other feedstocks into transportation fuels, including gasoline, diesel, jet fuel, ethanol, renewable diesel, and sustainable aviation fuel. The company is also the world's 2nd largest corn ethanol producer and a major renewable diesel producer through its Diamond Green Diesel joint venture.
